Machine learning

Machine learning is a powerful and evolving field that empowers computers to learn from data and improve their performance without being explicitly programmed. By analyzing patterns and relationships, ML systems can make predictions, detect anomalies, or even recognize speech and images. At its core, machine learning is divided into key branches like supervised learning (where models learn from labeled data), unsupervised learning (which finds hidden structures in unlabeled data), and reinforcement learning (where agents learn by interacting with environments).
